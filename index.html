Our Facility

A Renovated Community Gem

The Somerset Independent Alumni & Aquatic Center, formerly known as SomerFit, was purchased by the Somerset Independent School District in April 2018 and fully renovated to serve both the school's swim teams and the broader public.

The district invested in comprehensive renovations including updated pool equipment, reroofing, and modern interior finishes — transforming it into a premier fitness and aquatics destination for Pulaski County.
